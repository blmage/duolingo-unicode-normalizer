Whether to automatically normalize answers on each keystroke.

Pros

  • Safer with regards to further Duolingo updates.
  • Fixes rendering of diacritics on some browsers.

Cons

  • Compound characters such as "ậ", "ệ", "ộ́", etc., now count as 1 character instead of 2, unlike usual.
    This means that, for example, one backspace stroke suffices to remove both the letter and the diacritic.
